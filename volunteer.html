Volunteer Roles


Animal Care:

Assist with feeding, grooming, and walking our animals. Time commitment: 2-4 hours per week.

Shelter Maintenance:

Help keep our shelter clean and organized. Tasks include cleaning enclosures, organizing supplies, and general maintenance.

Event Support:

Help organize and run adoption events, fundraising activities, and community outreach programs.

Administrative Tasks:

Support the administrative team with tasks such as filing, data entry, and managing adoption paperwork.

Educational Outreach:

Help with educational programs that teach the community about responsible pet ownership and the importance of spaying/neutering.
