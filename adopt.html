Adoption Requirement

1. Adopter must age above 18

2. Malaysia citizen

3. Each adopter only can adopt 3 animals



Adoption Prosedure



1. Choose the animal that you wish to adopt.

2. Fill the adoption form below.

3. Having short interview about yourself on phone. This interview to ensure that our pets will be in goos hands when adopted.

4. We also allow walk-in adoptions.



Things that need to consider before adopt animals :

Readiness to treat : having the, commitment and resources to treat cats adequately

Qualification of residence : provide a safe and suitable environment for cats

Availability of resources: Having enough funds for the cost of food, medical care, and other supplies.

Knowledge about cat care : understand cat care needs such as food, worm treatment, vaccination, and dental care.

Family and household situation : ensure that family members and household situation support the adoption and well-being of animals.

Property owner consent : if you rent a residence, make sure that the property owner allows the adoption of pets.



Each pet adoption organization may have additional requirements that must be met, such as a home survey or interview. Be sure to understand the specific requirements of the adoption place before deciding to adopt animals in our pet's center.

Furry Family Heaven receives no government funding. As the donations we receive from adoptions and surrenders alone are not enough to cover the costs of caring for the animals here, we also raise funds through other channels such as charitable events to ensure the welfare of the animals at Furry Family Heaven.

Donations in cash and in kind are greatly appreciated. For a list of items we need at Furry Family Heaven, kindly refer to the Donate section.

Your help can make a difference. Thank you!

IMPORTANT

No animal is to be surrendered to or adopted from the shelter on credit.
Payment must be made and a receipt obtained.
Always insist on a receipt for any money that you hand over to our office personnel.

We do not hold animals or take payment to reserve animals.
We adopt strictly on a first-come, first-served basis.

Furry Family Heaven no longer holds animals as many animals who were put on hold by potential adopters were never adopted.
We want to find the best loving home for each animal so it is important for us to go through the full adoption process with each animal and potential adopter.
