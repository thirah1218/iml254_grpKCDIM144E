  • If your adopted animal becomes sick shortly after adoption, it's essential to contact us promptly. Despite the thorough health checks and vaccinations animals receive before adoption, some illnesses may not immediately manifest or could be incubating. FurryFamilyHeaven offer a health guarantee for a limited period, allowing you to return the pet for a refund or receive veterinary care at reduced costs. It's crucial to act quickly, as early detection and treatment often lead to successful outcomes.

  • Yes. Indeed you can. FurryFamilyHeaven have the expertise and resources to safely handle and care for stray animals. By bringing a stray animal to us, you give them a chance to be reunited with their owner if they have one, or to receive necessary medical care, food, and shelter until they can find a new home. This ensures the animal's well-being and increases the likelihood of a positive outcome for them. Additionally, reporting a stray animal helps ensure public safety and compliance with local animal welfare laws.

  • When visiting FurryFamilyHeaven, it's important to adhere to our regulations to ensure the safety and well-being of both the animals and visitors. Common regulations include:
    🐾 Following designated visiting hours and procedures for interacting with animals.
    🐾 Bringing a valid ID and completing any necessary paperwork or applications for adoption or volunteering.
    🐾 Following instructions from shelter staff regarding hygiene, such as hand sanitization and wearing appropriate footwear.
    🐾 Adhering to any vaccination or health requirements for your own pets if bringing them along.
    🐾 Respecting posted signage and guidelines regarding animal handling and behavior.

  • 🐾 Animal Caregiver. Feeding, cleaning and maintaining living spaces, grooming animals, and providing enrichment through play and exercise.
    🐾 Dog Walker. Walking dogs, training dogs, and observing and reporting any health or behavioural issues.
    🐾 Cat Socializer. Spending time with cats, playing with cats, and observing and reporting any health or behavioural issues.
    🐾 Transport Volunteer. Transporting animals from veterinary appointmnets to foster home or adoption events, ensuring the safe and comfortable transport of animals, and maintaining and cleaning transport equipment.
    🐾 Maintennace and Cleaning Volunteer. Performing regular cleaning tasks, helping with landscaping and outdoor upkeep, and assisting with the maintenance and repair of shelter facilities.

  • 🐾 Veterinary care. All animals in FurryFamilyHeaven are spayed or neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped before the adoption. They also receive a thorough health check by our veterinarians, ensuring they are healthy and ready for their new warm homes.
    🐾 Behavioral Assessment. Each animal undergoes a behavioral evaluation to help match them with the right home and provide adopters with insights into their new pet's temperament and needs.
    🐾 Pet Insurance Trial. FurryFamilyHeaven offer a trial period of pet isurance to cover any unexpected medical expenses during the first few weeks of adoption.
    🐾 Starter Kit. A basic supply of food to help your pet transition to their new diet at home. For dogs, this includes a leash and a collar; for cats, a litter box and litter.
